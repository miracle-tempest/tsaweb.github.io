Classes
Mountain Vista High School offers a variety of classes to teach students success skills. The engineering classes teach students to problem solve and refine their own designs. Business and marketing classes teach students money management skills, as well as how to start and grow a business. The technology classes teach students how to efficiently use computers, which is an important skill to have in our modern world. Between 6 knowledgeable teachers, 29 business and tech subjects are covered at MVHS.
